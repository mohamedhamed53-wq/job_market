💼 Job Market Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the job market, including job trends, required skills, top hiring companies, salary insights, and remote work opportunities. The project helps understand market demand and supports data-driven career and hiring decisions.

🧰 Tools & Technologies
Microsoft Power BI
Excel
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query

🌍 Remote vs On-site Jobs Analysis
💰 Salary Insights & Comparison
📈 Job Market Growth Trends
🏷️ Industry-wise Job Distribution
📈 Key Insights
Certain skills are consistently in high demand across multiple job roles.
Tech-related roles dominate the job market compared to other industries.
Remote jobs are increasing but still vary by industry and role.
Salary levels differ significantly based on skill set and experience.
A small number of companies contribute to a large share of job postings.
🎯 Project Goals
Analyze real job market data
Identify skill gaps and demand trends
Understand hiring patterns across companies
Support career planning and recruitment decisions
Build an interactive BI dashboard for insights
